Digital Multimeter

Procedure
  1. Ensure that the power in the circuit under measure is OFF. Ohmmeter applies external current. It can interact with the power in the circuit and damage it.
  2. Connect Black lead to COM.
  3. Connect Red lead to terminal with symbol .
  4. Press button Ω.
  5. Value of display is Resistance.
    • Example: Short circuiting leads should result in a very low value of Resistance.
