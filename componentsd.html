Digital Multimeter

Components
  • Agilent multimeter 34401A
  • Diodes: 1BH62, 1N3661, IN3893, 1N3494, 1N4001
  • Transistors: 2N3904, 2N3019, 2N3393, BC107BP
  • Resistors: 1 ΚΩ, 1.24 ΚΩ, 1.6 ΚΩ, 3.9 ΚΩ, 4.7 ΚΩ, 8.2 ΚΩ, 10 ΚΩ, 16 ΚΩ, 24 ΚΩ Milliammeter.
  • Measurement capability • 6½ digit resolution • 10 measurement functions: DC/AC voltage, DC/AC current, 2- and 4-wire resistance, diode, continuity, frequency, period • Basic accuracy: 0.0035% DC, 0.06% AC • 1000 V max voltage input, 3 A max current input
