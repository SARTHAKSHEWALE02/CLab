Hardness of Water

Procedure
      To find out the hardness of water sample: -
  • Pipette out 10 ml of given water sample in a conical flask, Add 5ml of buffer solution by test tube.
  • Add 2-3 drops of Eriochrome black -T indicator.
  • Titrate the water sample against EDTA solution.
  • The end point of this titration is wine red to blue, Repeat same procedure three times and note down the mean burette reading as 'X' ml.
  • From this reading find out the hardness of water sample in ppm.
  • Formula
    i) M++ + EBT ⟶ M-EBT (wine-red)+ 2H+
    ii) M++ + EDTA ⟶ M-EDTA +2H+ (Colorless)[with pH 10]
    iii) M-EBT + EDTA ⟶ M-EDTA + EBT (Blue).
