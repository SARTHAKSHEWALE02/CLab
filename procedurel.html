Polarization of light

Procedure
  • Enter the value of angle of Polarization in the box given blow Polariser.
  • Enter the value of angle of Analyser in the box given blow Analyser.
  • Note the value of Ratio of output intensity to input intensity of the Laser light.
  • Enter the value of angle of Analyser, angle of Polariser and ratio of output to input intensity.
  • Repeat the observation to complete the observation table.
  • Calculate the slope of the graph.
  • I=I0cos²θ Where, I0is intensity of plane polarized light incident on the Analyzer (constant) θ is angle between planes of transmission of polarizer and Analyzer, I is intensity of the emergent light
